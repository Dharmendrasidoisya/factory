Welcome to Marut Air Ventilation Engineers

At Marut Air Ventilation Engineers, we are proud to be one of India’s leading manufacturers of HVLS (High Volume Low Speed) Fans, delivering advanced ventilation solutions that elevate comfort, efficiency, and air quality across large-scale facilities.

Founded in 2016, our company brings together a dynamic team of architects, engineers, and product innovators who specialize in designing customized ventilation systems for diverse building applications—from industrial warehouses to commercial complexes.

What We Offer

- HVLS Fans – engineered for maximum airflow and minimal energy consumption

- Exhaust Fans – built for durability and high-performance extraction

- Mobile Fans– portable, powerful, and perfect for dynamic environments

- Industrial Coolers – designed to deliver efficient cooling in expansive spaces
